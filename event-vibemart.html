About Us

Sliceledger is a public blockchain protocol that uses a Proof of Stake and Proof of Play consensus mechanism. It offers developers a secure and scalable platform to build decentralized applications with low transaction fees. It is open to the public, allowing anyone to participate in securing the network and verifying the record of transactions. This ensures that the network is transparent and tamper-proof, and that all participants have a stake in its success. By providing a secure, scalable, and affordable platform for decentralized applications, Sliceledger can help to create a more open, transparent, and decentralized digital world.
